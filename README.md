# Equity Research Report & Valuation of Asian Paints Ltd.

> A professional equity research project focused on fundamental analysis, financial statement analysis, valuation, and investment recommendation for Asian Paints Limited.

---

## Overview

This repository contains a comprehensive equity research report on **Asian Paints Limited**, one of India's leading decorative paint manufacturers.

The objective of this project is to replicate the workflow followed by professional Equity Research Analysts by performing:

- Business Analysis
- Industry Analysis
- Financial Statement Analysis
- Ratio Analysis
- Discounted Cash Flow (DCF) Valuation
- Comparable Company Analysis (CCA)
- Investment Thesis
- Risk Analysis
- Final Investment Recommendation

This project is being developed as part of my journey towards Investment Banking, Equity Research, Corporate Finance, and Capital Allocation.

---

## Objectives

- Understand the business model of Asian Paints
- Analyse the Indian Paint Industry
- Study historical financial performance
- Build a complete DCF valuation model
- Perform Comparable Company Analysis
- Estimate intrinsic value
- Develop an investment thesis
- Present findings in a professional equity research report

## Valuation Methodology

### Discounted Cash Flow (DCF)

The valuation model includes:

- Revenue Forecasting
- Margin Assumptions
- Capital Expenditure
- Working Capital Forecast
- Free Cash Flow to Firm (FCFF)
- Weighted Average Cost of Capital (WACC)
- Terminal Value
- Sensitivity Analysis

---

### Comparable Company Analysis

Key valuation multiples include:

- EV / EBITDA
- EV / Sales
- P/E Ratio
- Price / Book
- PEG Ratio
